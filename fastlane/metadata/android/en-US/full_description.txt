Just Listen is a free and open-source music player client. It uses the Audius public APIs to search, stream, and play music.

Features:
- Search songs & playlists on the Audius infrastructure
- Listen to songs and audiobooks in the background for free
- Create your private playlists
- Add songs to favorites
- Track recent play history and most listened songs
- Set a sleep timer to close the app automatically
- Different UI themes
- Shuffle and repeat play modes
- Seamless transition between songs
- Built with Kotlin Multiplatform

Note: This FOSS version includes support features that are compliant with open-source guidelines. Streaming high-quality audio may consume significant network data.
